# Lumina Coffee Systems — Company Overview

Lumina Coffee Systems builds connected espresso machines for offices and
hotels. Every machine streams telemetry (water temperature, extraction time,
bean levels) to the Lumina Cloud, where facility managers monitor fleets in
real time.

Founded in 2019, Lumina operates in 14 countries and maintains a fleet of
over 40,000 machines. Our flagship model is the Lumina S3; the compact
Lumina Go targets small offices under 20 people.
