# Pricing

Lumina offers three subscription tiers, billed per machine per month:

- **Starter** — 49 EUR/month: one Lumina Go, cloud telemetry, email support.
- **Team** — 129 EUR/month: one Lumina S3, telemetry, priority support,
  free bean delivery once a month.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing: fleet management console, SLA 99.9%,
  dedicated account manager.

All tiers include a 30-day free trial. Hardware remains the property of
Lumina and is replaced free of charge within 48 hours if it fails.
