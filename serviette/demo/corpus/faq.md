# Frequently Asked Questions

**Which beans can I use?** Any beans work, but Lumina Select beans are
calibrated per machine model and give the most consistent extraction.

**What happens if a machine goes offline?** Machines keep brewing offline;
telemetry buffers locally for up to 7 days and syncs when connectivity
returns.

**Can I cancel anytime?** Yes. Subscriptions are monthly; cancellation takes
effect at the end of the billing period, and we collect the hardware within
two weeks.
